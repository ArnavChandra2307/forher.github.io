My Dearest Anokhi,

From the first moment I really saw you, something inside me softened and quietly decided: that I wanna spend my whole life with you.

Since then, the days have been stitched together with small, golden threads — your laugh on sleepy calls, your voice when you're excited, the way you say my name when you're soft. I fall for you over and over when you get mad at me for doing somethin' stupid (jo ki hamesha mere se expected hota hai 😅)

You are not just the pretty moments; you are the storms and the quiet after, the way you pick yourself up, the way you carry the world and still manage to bring light into mine. I admire you most when you are still in your period cramps and still wanna make yourself talk to me, rather than resting. That is not small — that is everything.

There are corners of my future I can't see clearly yet, but every version that feels like home has you in it. In those quiet daydreams, we're waking up together in our room, cuddling in each other's arms — because why can't we just do that?

I know I won't always get everything right. I'll fumble, I'll learn, I'll grow — but I promise to keep choosing you on the days that are easy and on the days that are heavy. I want you to remember that, even when I'm quiet, I am still right by your side — to love you, to support you, and to carry you in my heart.

Thank you for being the girl who turned ordinary days into something I ache to write about, who made the calendar feel like it finally had a meaning. Above everything, you are one of the most precious things I received from Bhagwan Ji — and I am so endlessly, softly grateful that our paths found each other.

With all of my love,
Arnav 🌸
